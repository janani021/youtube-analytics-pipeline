YouTube Channel Performance Dashboard 

This dashboard provides a view of how the YouTube channel  (MKBHD) performed over the most recent time. 


The goal is to understand:
- How the channel performed today vs yesterday
- Which videos contributed most to growth
- Which videos are trending right now
- Overall engagement of the channel

1. KPI Summary Row
The KPI row shows three key performance:
- Total Views
- Total Likes
- Overall Engagement Rate

Each KPI includes:
- Latest dayâ€™s total value
- Up/Down arrow showing if the metric improved vs previous day
- Percentage change vs previous day

2. Daily View Change (Last 7 Days)
Instead of showing total views per day, this focuses on the actual daily increase or decrease in views*.

This helps answer:
- How many views did we gain today?
- Whether the channel is trending up or down recently

3. Video Stats 
This section displays each video's latest stats as a combination of bars and a heat-style layout. 
For each video, it shows:
- View Count
- Like Count
- Engagement Rate

You can easily compare videos and see which ones have strong engagement even if views are lower.

4. Top 5 Videos
This highlights the top 5 videos based on total view count. 

- Which topics are attracting the most with the audience


5. Trending Videos (Last 7 Days)
This lollipop-style visualization shows the videos that gained the most views in the past 7 days.
It highlights the videos that are currently trending or getting more attention.

It helps answer:
- "What are viewers watching the most this week?"
- "Which videos should we focus on or promote?"
